• Scientific Name: Equus zebra
  • Habitat: Mostly found in African grasslands and savannas
  • Nickname: "Striped Horse" due to its black-and-white pattern
  • Social Behavior: Lives in herds for protection
  • Speed: Can run up to 65 km/h
  • Strength: Strong legs, can kick predators like lions
  • Lifespan: 20-25 years in the wild, up to 30 years in captivity
  • Diet: Herbivorous, mainly eats grass and leaves
