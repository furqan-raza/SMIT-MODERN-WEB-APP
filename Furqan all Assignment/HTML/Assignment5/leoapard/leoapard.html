• Scientific Name: Panthera pardus
  • Habitat: Found in forests, grasslands, mountains, and even deserts
  • Nickname: "Silent Predator" due to its stealthy hunting skills
  • Social Behavior: Mostly solitary and territorial
  • Speed: Can run up to 58 km/h
  • Strength: One of the strongest big cats, can carry prey up trees
  • Lifespan: 12-17 years in the wild, up to 23 years in captivity
  • Diet: Carnivorous, hunts deer, monkeys, and small mammals
