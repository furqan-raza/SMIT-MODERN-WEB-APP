• Scientific Name: Loxodonta
  • Habitat: Found in Africa and Asia, prefers forests and grasslands
  • Nickname: "Gentle Giant"
  • Social Behavior: Lives in herds, led by a matriarch
  • Size: Largest land animal, can grow up to 4 meters tall
  • Weight: Can weigh between 2,700 to 6,000 kg
  • Lifespan: 60-70 years
  • Diet: Herbivore, eats grass, fruits, and bark
