• Scientific Name: Vulpes vulpes
  • Habitat: Found in forests, grasslands, mountains, and even urban areas
  • Nickname: "Cunning Creature" due to its intelligence and trickery
  • Social Behavior: Mostly solitary but sometimes lives in small family groups
  • Speed: Can run up to 50 km/h
  • Strength: Excellent hunter with sharp eyesight and hearing
  • Lifespan: 3-4 years in the wild, up to 14 years in captivity
  • Diet: Omnivorous, eats small animals, fruits, and insects
