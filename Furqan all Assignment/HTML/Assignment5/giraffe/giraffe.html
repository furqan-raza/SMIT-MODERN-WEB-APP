• Scientific Name: Giraffa camelopardalis
  • Habitat: Found in African savannas and open woodlands
  • Nickname: "Tallest Mammal"
  • Height: Can grow up to 5.5 meters tall
  • Tongue Length: Up to 50 cm, helps in reaching high branches
  • Speed: Can run up to 60 km/h in short bursts
  • Lifespan: 25 years in the wild, longer in captivity
  • Diet: Herbivore, mainly eats leaves and fruits
