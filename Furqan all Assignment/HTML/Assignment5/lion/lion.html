• Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Habitat: Mostly found in Africa's grasslands and forests
  • Nickname: "King of the Jungle" but actually lives in open lands
  • Social Animal: Lives in groups called "prides" (10-15 lions)
  • Powerful Roar: Can be heard up to 8 km away
  • Hunting: Lionesses do most of the hunting
  • Speed: Can run up to 80 km/h in short distances
  • Lifespan: 10-14 years in the wild, up to 20 years in captivity
  • Diet: Carnivorous, mainly hunts zebras, buffaloes, and antelopes
