• Scientific Name: Equus ferus caballus
  • Habitat: Found in grasslands, farms, and mountains
  • Nickname: "Man's Best Helper" due to their use in farming & transportation
  • Social Animals: Lives in herds with a leader
  • Speed: Can run up to 88 km/h (fastest recorded)
  • Strength: Can carry heavy loads and pull carts
  • Lifespan: 25-30 years
  • Diet: Herbivorous, mainly eats grass, hay, and grains
